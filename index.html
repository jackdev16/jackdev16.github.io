WHY HIRE A SOCIAL MEDIA MANAGER?

BRAND AWARENESS

The key benefits of social media boil down to branding and awareness. In short, people want to know what you're all about before making a purchase. If you're a local business people are likely to look you up via social media to check your hours, location, services, or even to just nosey on your reviews, etc... This is why it's so important to have good brand awareness through your social platforms.

GROWTH

The term "you've got to spend money to make money" is unfortunately in fact 100% true. Every time any of my clients or even, just friends or people I know have invested in their businesses, they have reaped the rewards tenfold. Whether it be investing in products to help run your business more smoothly, hiring a social media manager, or just outsourcing specific tasks. spending the money is worth it.

FAST CUSTOMER SERVICE

Social media provides a constant and easy point of contact between you and your customers. The ability to respond quickly and in real-time prevents potential customers from going to a competitor. While this is not easy when you are also running a business and wearing a million hats. This is exactly why a social media manager can be extremely helpful.

MORE TIME

Your focus should be on what you do best. Outsourcing tasks or hiring a social media manager will give you the spare time to focus on what you are best at which increases your productivity. If you're spending time on tasks that you don't like or that you are maybe not very good at then you're wasting precious time which could be spent on other tasks to build your business!
